JSFoo 2013

20th — 21st September 2013

MLR Convention Centre, J. P. Nagar

Conference & Workshops on JavaScript in Bangalore

JSFoo is back! India’s first JavaScript conference returns for a third edition after two successful runs in 2011 and 2012. This year’s event will focus on original creations with JavaScript. Come join us.

Previously at JSFoo

  • Bangalore 2012
  • Chennai 2012
  • Pune 2012
  • Bangalore 2011

The first season of JSFoo featured editions in Bangalore, Pune and Chennai between Oct 2011 and Feb 2012. The second edition in Bangalore in Oct 2012 was based on the theme “JavaScript Everywhere” — featuring JavaScript on web servers, embedded in robots, as a cross-platform language for mobile, and in its original home, the browser.
